Chamber Music Players return to the Blue Bamboo for an intimate afternoon of richly textured chamber music. This program brings together luminous works written for small, conversational ensembles—Antonín Dvořák’s warmly expressive Terzetto in C Major and Moritz Moszkowski’s dazzling Suite for Two Violins and Piano.

Central Florida Jazz Society presents: Palm Beach Society Orchestra

Sun, Apr 12 @ 3:00PM — 5:00PM (EDT, UTC-04)

An Afternoon of American Classics, Disney Magic, and Big Band Jazz.

The Grand Floridian Society Orchestra for decades was a beloved six-piece musical ensemble performing at Disney’s Grand Floridian Resort & Spa in Walt Disney World. They are renowned for their classic swing, jazz, Disney tunes, and popular standards. The orchestra provided an elegant and nostalgic soundtrack for resort guests and brought a sense of charm to every performance. The transformation from the Grand Floridian Society Orchestra to the Palm Beach Society Orchestra stands as a testament to the resilience and passion of the musicians, ensuring that their legacy of live, sophisticated music continues to delight audiences. During this performance, the Palm Beach Society Orchestra will offer a vibrant selection of their music that spans genres and eras. You can expect a lively performance featuring classics from the American Songbook to Disney tunes and jazz hits from the big band era.

Florida Smooth Jazz Foundation presents: Rick Braun with special guest Randy Jacobs

Sun, May 3 @ 7:00PM — 9:00PM (EDT, UTC-04)

Rick Braun, with 30 #1 Billboard hits, is an award-winning trumpeter, composer, and producer with a career that has made him one of contemporary jazz’s most celebrated and popular artists. He began his career in the ‘80s as a singer and songwriter, writing REO Speedwagon’s top 20 hit “Here with Me” and the theme for NBC’s Midnight Caller. He later returned to the trumpet, touring and recording with Rod Stewart, Sade, Tina Turner, Tom Petty, Natalie Cole, Glenn Frey and War, and with the combo groups BWB (Braun, Kirk Whalum and Norman Brown) and RnR (Braun and Richard Elliot). As a solo artist, Braun has released acclaimed albums including Beat Street, Kisses in the Rain, All It Takes, and Can You Feel It. Influenced by legends Miles Davis, Herb Alpert, Freddie Hubbard and Chuck Mangione, Braun’s music combines soul, melody, and sophistication.

Randy Jacobs, world renowned guitarist and leader of the Boneshakers, the band that blends funk, rock and blues with vocalist Jenny Langer, cowrote the 1987 hit “Walk the Dinosaur” while a member of Was (Not Was) with Don Was. He has toured and recorded with numerous artists including Bonnie Raitt, BB King, Seal, Kris Kristofferson, Tears for Fears, Paula Abdul, Euge Groove, Warren Hill, Mindi Abair, and Rick Braun, and he is the current Music Director for Dave Koz including the Dave Koz Christmas tour, Dave Koz Summer Horns, and Dave Koz Cruises. Rick and Randy are joined by an all-star band including BK Jackson, Ron Reinhardt, Tim George, and Third Richardson. Don’t miss your chance to see this very special event following the sold-out Daytona Jazz Weekend in the intimate setting of the 188-seat Blue Bamboo Center for the Arts!

Tickets are $66 - $110 including processing fees
